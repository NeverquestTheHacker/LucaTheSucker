Those data, needed to use web services, are exclusively and only used to guarantee more security and stability for our systems, and so, for our users.
Sometimes, our systems can proceed to record more data than needed, but that doesn't apply to normal traffic, and so, to fair Users (Correct and allowed website usage). We define it "Security Mode". When our systems detect something strange (by analyzing the data mentioned above) in someone's traffic (generated as example by Bots, Botnets, Malwares, unallowed Scrapers, Malicious Users, Security Issues Scanners, Port Scanners), they start to collect all the data that will be useful in case of future Legal Actions or Security Patches.
